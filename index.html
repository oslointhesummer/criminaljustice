Who are Asian Americans?

Asian American is a heterogeneous group of people who traced their ancestry to one or more countries in Eastern, Southern and South-Eastern Asia. They have been in significant parts of the contemporary United States.

Now 6.5% of the U.S. population consists of Asians..
